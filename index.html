About

I am a biophysics PhD student in the Bi Group at Northeastern University. I am interested in tissue mechanics, rheology, and plasticity. My research focuses on using theoretical and computational models to understand how biological tissues respond to external stimuli — conditions commonly encountered in various physiological processes — as well as to internal changes arising from cell-cell interactions.
